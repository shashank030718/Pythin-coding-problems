What is Bubble Sort?

Bubble Sort is a simple sorting algorithm.

It works by:

👉 Comparing two nearby numbers
👉 If they are in the wrong order, we swap them

The bigger number moves to the right, just like a bubble going up in water.



arr = [7,2,5,3]

n = len(arr)

for i in range(n):

    for j in range(n-1):

        if arr[j] > arr[j+1]:

            arr[j], arr[j+1] = arr[j+1], arr[j]

print(arr)
